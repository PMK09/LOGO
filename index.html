About us

LOGO is a upcoming donation platform wants to spread our wings in a large scale to help the government by collecting funds from the Donors living in places throughout the country in this pandemic period. Through this platform you can either donate to central government or a particular state government you want to donate. Please join hands to donate and help the government as well the people in India who have lost their livelihood and are also in danger of getting in contact with Covid.
